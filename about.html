Think in Black and White... BW Lens Photography is owned and operated by Mary Jane a professional photograper for over 20 years. BW Lens Photography is a team of creative individuals that capture and create amazing memories of your life events, milestones, and forever memories. The services we offer is capture in black and white photography. Our creative photography style blends stunning portraiture with storytelling and has been our specialty for over 20 years. Our goal is to exceed your expectations and provide you with amazing memories to share with your family, friends and business. Our photographers are assisted by a diligent client services team, here to support you every step of the way. We pride ourselves on impeccable service and attention to detail, ensuring that your entire experience is just as flawless as your wedding photos.

Portraiture and Headshots - A big part of the portraits we provide are holiday photos for family gifts. We create custom holiday cards and seasonal press printed products. Thank you cards, invitations, save the date cards and any other reason you have to send a card... we can make it. Just ask and we can bring your idea to life. Maternity, BUMP and new born portraits can be scheduled in our portrait studio in New York or on location in your home or any place you choose. All types of family portraits-childrens portraits - maternity photos - newborn portraits - pet photography - school photos - communion photos - senior photography - dating profile photos - real estate photography and video.

Wedding and Event Services - BW Lens Photography Service specializes in Weddings, Bar and Bat Mitzvahs, Sweet 16 Celebrations and all other events you may celebrate. Our creative team will edited in a storybook style that will bring back the memories for years to come. Corporate-Business-Profile Photos - Headshots, portraits for your Linkedin page, corporate newsletters or any other type of professional portrait can be scheduled in our NY studio or we can come to you and set up on-location photography. Our photo team can work well with your social media campaigns and other corporate media needs that you are working on. Just contact us and we can discuss your project in detail.
